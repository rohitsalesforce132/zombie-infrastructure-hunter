## Methodology

Resources scored on 5 dimensions (0-20 each):
1. Documentation — Context graph node exists and fresh?
2. Ownership — Owner tag exists and owner active?
3. Dependencies — Other resources depend on this?
4. Activity — Recent changes or access in 90 days?
5. Incident Relevance — Ever involved in incidents?

Score 0-20 = 🧟 Tier 1 | 21-40 = 🧟‍♂️ Tier 2 | 41-60 = ⚠️ Tier 3 | 61-80 = 💤 Tier 4 | 81-100 = ✅ Active
